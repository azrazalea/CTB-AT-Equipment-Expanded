[h1]CTB-AT Equipment Expanded[/h1]

[b]Enhanced compatibility bridge between Colonist Tech Background and Arcane Technology[/b]

This mod ensures that pawns with different technological backgrounds can appropriately use equipment based on their knowledge level, creating a more immersive and balanced gameplay experience.

[h1]Features[/h1]
[list]
[*] Allows tribal pawns to use medieval equipment without research
[*] Enables industrial pawns to use modern weapons without researching them first
[*] Full support for Medieval Overhaul (160+ items)
[*] Simple Sidearms integration - replaces Arcane Technology's patch for enhanced tech background support
[*] These are Spacer Tech compatibility - automatic tech level adjustments
[*] Comprehensive equipment rules for vanilla and DLC items
[*] Smart tech level matching based on pawn backgrounds
[/list]

[h1]How It Works[/h1]
The mod bridges the gap between Colonist Tech Background (which gives pawns tech knowledge traits) and Arcane Technology (which locks equipment behind research). With this mod:

[list]
[*] [b]Tribal pawns[/b] can use simple medieval weapons and armor without research
[*] [b]Medieval pawns[/b] can use all medieval equipment plus basic firearms with research
[*] [b]Industrial pawns[/b] can use modern weapons without needing to research gunsmithing
[*] [b]Spacer pawns[/b] have access to advanced technology without research requirements
[/list]

[h1]Technical Details[/h1]
This mod uses Harmony patches to intercept equipment checks and apply custom tech level rules based on pawn backgrounds. It maintains compatibility with other mods by using a shared equipment checking system.
